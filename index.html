about hope

Hope-Marie David was born and raised in Vallejo, California.
She is a student at San Francisco State University pursuing a
Bachelors of Science degree in Speech-Language and Hearing Sciences.
She serves as the President of SFSU's Student Academy of Audiology chapter
and is a student researcher in the SFSU Find your Voice Lab
and the SFSU Audiology Lab on campus.
She loves to create art, read, and hang out with her loved ones
along with her cat, Neo!
